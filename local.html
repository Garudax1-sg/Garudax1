FAQ

Getting your product into Singapore stores

How do I get my product into stores in Singapore?

Garuda X1 sends field agents to visit stores on your behalf — from a simple namecard drop up to a full in-person pitch and follow-up with the decision-maker. You choose the depth of coverage and the batch size, starting from a minimum of 100 stores.

Does Garuda X1 take a sales commission?

No. We're paid per store visit, not on commission. You get a flat rate card and a weekly report — no cut taken from any resulting sales.

How many stores can you cover?

Engagements start at a minimum of 100 stores and scale up to 1,000+ store visits per batch — convenience stores, mini-marts and independent retailers islandwide.

Who is this service for?

Singapore-based F&B, FMCG, beverage and consumer goods brands that want their product physically placed and pitched into retail stores without building an in-house field sales team.
